I was raised in Portland, Oregon, and it is there I make my home base. I am insatiably curious and love to explore, so my free time is spent learning new skills and planning my next adventure.

I am most passionate about experiential travel that expands my understanding of other cultures and the ecological concerns of our planet. When I travel, I prefer local experiences to luxury- or all-inclusive packages curated for tourists.

When I am not designing and developing websites, you can find me guiding local, interpretive tours, making espresso drinks at the farmers market, and blogging about non-aspirational travel.
